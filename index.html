Inventory Management

QuickTrack is an inventory management website designed to streamline inventory processes for stores. It provides real-time tracking of stock levels, notifications for low inventory, and detailed analytics to help stores make informed decisions. Technologically, we can assist users by integrating mobile compatibility and APIs for syncing with existing systems to enhance user convenience and operational efficiency.

About Us

Our web-based inventory management system (WBIMS) was developed by a dedicated team of Software Engineering students at Babcock University, Nigeria, to address the critical inefficiencies faced by Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in managing their inventory. Inspired by Babcock University‚Äôs commitment to technological innovation and excellence, this project bridges the gap between outdated manual processes and overly complex digital solutions, offering a streamlined, user-friendly platform that empowers businesses to optimize stock levels, reduce operational costs, and enhance decision-making.


Why We Built This System Traditional inventory management methods, such as manual record-keeping or legacy software, often lead to errors, stock discrepancies, and delayed decision-making. SMEs, in particular, struggle with limited resources and technical expertise, making it challenging to adopt scalable solutions.

Our WBIMS tackles these issues head-on by:

  • - Automating workflows to eliminate human errors and save time.
  • - Providing real-time stock tracking across multiple locations.
  • - Sending automated email alerts for low stock levels and product expirations.
  • - Offering role-based access control to ensure data security and operational efficiency.
  • - Delivering comprehensive reports (PDF, CSV) for informed strategic planning.
